About the Project

Where life unfolds
at a different pace

Tierra Madre is a fully masterplanned residential development in Playa del Carmen, one of the Caribbean's most connected and desirable cities. Designed around the idea that everyday living should feel like a resort, every element — from the spa to the cenote cave, amphitheater to padel courts — has been woven into a single, cohesive community.

With Playa del Carmen's international airport minutes away and the Riviera Maya's beaches on your doorstep, this is living without compromise: full amenities, investment potential, and a lifestyle that most people only access on holiday.

The Club House

A resort-level
club at your door

The Club House is the beating heart of Tierra Madre — a resort-grade facility that residents access daily. The lobby spa, wellness zones, and social spaces are finished to a standard that rivals Mexico's finest boutique hotels.

From the tranquil lobby to the outdoor terraces, every space is designed for the moments that make life feel exceptional — morning coffee, evening cocktails, weekend gatherings.

Landscape & Art

Nature is the
architecture

Tierra Madre's open spaces are designed as a living landscape — sculpture gardens, reflective water features, shaded walkways, and green corridors connect every corner of the development. It is a community you want to walk through, not just live in.

Signature Feature

A private
cenote cave

One of Tierra Madre's most extraordinary features — a cave cenote experience within the development itself. The Yucatán Peninsula sits atop one of the world's largest underground river systems, and here, that natural wonder becomes part of daily life.

Cool, clear water. Natural stone. A moment of total stillness — right at home.
