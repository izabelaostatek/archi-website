Services

What I offer

Full-service architecture and interior design — from first sketch to finished space.

I work with private clients, developers, and hospitality groups on residential and commercial projects. Every project is different — scope, budget, location — but the approach is the same: understand what you actually need, then design something that delivers it properly.

01

Architecture

New builds, extensions, and renovations. I handle the full process — site analysis, concept design, planning applications, technical documentation, and construction oversight.

  • Site analysis & feasibility
  • Concept & schematic design
  • Planning & permits
  • Construction documentation
  • Project management
  • Sustainable design strategy
02

Interior Design

High-end residential and commercial interiors. From space planning to final styling — materials, furniture, lighting, and every detail in between.

  • Space planning & layout
  • Material & finish selection
  • Furniture & lighting design
  • Custom joinery & built-ins
  • Supplier coordination
  • Final styling & installation
03

Concept & Vision

Early-stage design direction for projects that need clarity before they commit to a full brief. Visual concept development, mood direction, and spatial strategy.

  • Concept development
  • Visual direction & mood
  • 3D visualisations & renders
  • Spatial strategy
  • Wellness space design
  • Design for developers

Process

How it works

01

Discovery

We talk through your project, site, timeline, and budget. No fluff — just what's needed to understand the brief.

02

Concept

Initial design directions with visuals, references, and spatial ideas. You'll see the thinking early, so we can align before committing.

03

Design

Full technical and design development. Plans, elevations, material specifications — everything needed to build.

04

Delivery

On-site coordination and oversight through to completion. The design stays intact from drawing to finished space.
