In the heart of
the ancient Maya jungle

"The harmony of being in your place of peace, in a space that embraces you and won't let you go."

OKOM takes its name from an atypical wood found only in the Riviera Maya — the ancient timber used to hold up the thatched rooftops of Maya construction. Here, it becomes a philosophy: something that holds you up, roots you, and shelters you in the most beautiful way.

Located in the heart of Tulum — five minutes from the beach clubs and hotel zone, steps from the best restaurants and cenotes — OKOM is a boutique residential development where biophilic architecture meets genuinely effortless living. Cascading tropical plants, natural stone, and light-washed interiors create a building that feels as alive as the jungle surrounding it.

Sky pool and rooftop grill. Private cinema. A coffee shop at your door. Yoga and wellness. Resort-level service, all day. This is Tulum at its finest.

The Name

A word rooted in
Maya tradition

OKOM is the name of a rare, atypical wood found only in the Riviera Maya — used by the ancient Maya to build the timber beams that held up the thatched rooftops of their homes. Strong, rare, irreplaceable.

For this project, OKOM becomes a philosophy. It is the pillar that holds everything up — the structure beneath a life well lived. A home that doesn't just shelter you, but sustains you.

The Vision

Natural and modern,
in equal measure

OKOM sits in the heart of Tulum — one of the world's most sought-after destinations — but it doesn't feel like a resort development. It feels like a place that belongs here. The architecture is biophilic: cascading tropical plants, natural stone textures, light and air flowing through every space.

Inside, the residences are calm and considered — light wood, natural tones, open layouts that invite you to stop, rest, and feel genuinely at home. The whole building is designed to make you feel like you've arrived somewhere that was waiting for you.
