Where Tulum's soul
meets refined living

"Your own paradise"

Ardeh Tulum is a boutique residential development conceived around one idea: that a home should feel like paradise. Warm stone, tropical planting, arched doorways and cascading greenery create a building that breathes with the landscape around it.

From the hand-finished lobby to the rooftop pool and bar, every space at Ardeh has been designed with the detail and intention of a luxury boutique hotel — and the warmth and privacy of a home. A short drive from the beach clubs, the Gran Cenote, and the Tulum ruins, this is Tulum at its most considered.
