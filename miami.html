Project Acqualina Mansions
Location Miami, Florida
Type Interior Design
Style Luxury Residential
Year 2021

Oceanfront living at
Acqualina Mansions

A full interior design concept for a private villa at Acqualina Mansions, one of Miami's most prestigious waterfront addresses. The project spans the main living and entertaining floors — each space designed around the ocean views that define the building.

The palette is warm ivory, champagne and gold throughout, with accents of teal and deep navy in the living areas. Roberto Cavalli furniture anchors every room — tufted sofas, sculptural dining chairs, bespoke lighting — selected for their presence and their ability to hold a large, light-filled space without disappearing into it.

The lobby and reception area introduces a signature biophilic gesture: a curved living green wall behind the reception desk, sculptural staircase in white marble, and layered gold pendant lighting that sets the tone for the residential floors above. The master bedroom is calm and enveloping — textured wallcovering, an oversized upholstered headboard, and a leopard-print rug that grounds the space.
