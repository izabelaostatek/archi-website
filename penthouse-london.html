Project St. George Penthouse
Location London, UK
Type Interior Design
Style Luxury Residential
Year 2020

Luxury penthouse interior with
Roberto Cavalli furnishings

A full interior design concept for a penthouse in London's prestigious St. George development. The brief called for a high-end residential interior — considered, luxurious, and coherent across every room.

The design draws on a warm palette of champagne, deep green, and polished gold. Roberto Cavalli furniture anchors each space — the iconic Blake sofa and Manhattan pieces in the living room, velvet dining chairs that bring colour without competing with the architecture. Materials were selected for texture and longevity: book-matched stone, botanical wallcoverings, and bespoke joinery throughout.

The floor plan was developed to maximise the open living and dining zone while maintaining clear separation between the bedroom suite and entertaining areas — a common challenge in large-format penthouse layouts.
