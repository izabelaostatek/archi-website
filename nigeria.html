Project Family Residence
Location Lagos, Nigeria
Type Architecture & Interiors
Style Contemporary African
Scope Full Design — Concept to Completion
Year 2019

A contemporary family home
rooted in African identity

A large-scale residential project in Lagos — a full family home designed from the ground up with a Contemporary African design language. The brief was ambitious: a home that felt unmistakably luxurious, functional for a large family, and grounded in African material culture without falling back on cliché.

The ground floor organises a generous living, dining and bar area around the pool deck, with a dedicated arts room, guest suite, kitchen and service areas. The first floor separates the private family living zone from the master suite, children's rooms, prayer room and a fully specified media room — all developed with detailed furniture and material specifications per room.

Interior concepts were presented as full room-by-room mood boards with Roberto Cavalli Home furniture selections, custom material palettes, and bespoke wallcovering proposals. Every space was specified: from the Aruba modular sofa in elephant leather to the Maui bar cabinet in black lacquer with gold leaf detailing.
