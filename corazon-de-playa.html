Corazón de Playa

Boutique residences in the heart of Playa del Carmen — where biophilic design, rooftop living and the Caribbean spirit converge in one extraordinary building.

The Project

Live anywhere.
Work from paradise.

Corazón de Playa is designed for a new kind of resident — the remote worker, the digital nomad, the creative professional who refuses to choose between career and lifestyle. In the heart of Playa del Carmen, steps from Quinta Avenida and minutes from the Caribbean, this is where your best work happens.

A co-working studio, high-speed connectivity, a neon-lit rooftop pool, a serious gym, and private terraces with plunge pools. Everything you need to be productive by day and completely alive by night — without leaving the building.

Studios and one-bedroom apartments designed for those who have realised that the office was always optional — and the Caribbean was always the answer.
