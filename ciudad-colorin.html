A city designed for
the way life should feel

Ciudad Colorín is Mérida's first Smart & Conscious City — a master-planned development that combines technology, nature, security, and wellness into a single extraordinary place. Not a suburb. Not a gated estate. A whole city built with intention.

Set in the fastest-growing city in Mexico, ranked #1 for quality of life by the UN, Ciudad Colorín offers more than a property. It offers a way of life — where world-class amenities, green open spaces, and smart infrastructure create a community unlike anything else in the Yucatán.

With 11 distinct districts spread across 392 acres, the development is designed at the scale of a true neighbourhood — with its own central park, cultural venues, sports facilities, schools, and a private club that rivals the best resorts in Mexico.
