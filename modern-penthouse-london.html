Project Modern Penthouse
Location London, UK
Type Interior Design
Style Modern Luxury
Year 2025

A modern London penthouse
with no compromises

A full interior design concept for a luxury penthouse in London. The brief was uncompromising — every room fully resolved, every material considered, and a clear point of view carried through from entrance to bedroom.

The main living area anchors the scheme: deep grey modular sofas, animal-print cushions, and a dramatic book-matched stone feature wall that draws the eye across the full depth of the space. Sculptural silver ottomans ground the seating arrangement without breaking the open-plan flow.

The dining and lounge zone continues with dark Emperador marble, a sculptural pendant cluster, and curved architectural ceilings that give the space a sense of movement. The master bedroom shifts register entirely — deep teal velvet, a Roberto Cavalli blue leather headboard, an art deco dressing room with illuminated wardrobes, and a fur-trimmed cushion palette that makes the room feel genuinely private and indulgent.
