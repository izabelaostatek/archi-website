Project Apartment Renovation
Location London, UK
Type Interior Design
Style Modern Luxury
Year 2020

A compact London apartment
designed to feel larger

A full interior design renovation for a private apartment in London. The brief was to create a space that felt genuinely luxurious within a compact footprint — layered, considered, and with a strong point of view.

The living and dining zone is anchored by a botanical wallcovering and gold vertical detailing — a bold backdrop that gives the open-plan space a clear identity. Chartreuse dining chairs bring colour and energy without overwhelming the palette, balanced by the softer tones of the lounge area and the city views beyond.

The bedroom takes a darker, moodier direction — dark textured wallcovering, a leopard-print rug, and jewel-toned accents in aubergine and charcoal. The result is a space that feels private and enveloping, a clear counterpoint to the brightness of the living areas. Every room was fully specified, from furniture and lighting to art, textiles, and accessories.
