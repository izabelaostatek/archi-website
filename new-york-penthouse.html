Project Luxury Penthouse
Location New York, USA
Type Interior Design
Style Modern Luxury
Year 2024

Dark, considered luxury
above the city

A full interior design concept for a luxury penthouse in New York — dramatic, deeply considered, and unapologetically moody. The brief called for an interior that felt genuinely private: a retreat above the city rather than a showpiece for it.

The open-plan living and kitchen zone anchors the scheme in darkness — charcoal and slate tones, a deep blue velvet sofa, and a sculptural water feature wall that brings movement and calm to the heart of the space. Roberto Cavalli furnishings carry through every room, selected for their tactile presence and their ability to hold a large, high-ceilinged floor plate.

The master bedroom is oriented around the city skyline — a full-width glazed wall frames the Manhattan sunset as a living artwork, with a low-profile bed and layered textiles that draw the eye outward. A study suite anchored by an oversized graphic rug completes the layout, giving the apartment a sense of both drama and retreat.
