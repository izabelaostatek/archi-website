About the work

Izabela Ostatek — Architect & Interior Designer

Architecture is about
how people live

I'm an architect and interior designer based in London, working on residential projects and high-end interiors across Europe and beyond. My practice sits at the intersection of sustainable design and luxury — buildings that perform well, look considered, and feel genuinely good to be inside.

I work across the full project lifecycle — from first concept through planning, documentation, and delivery. I use AI tools and online CAD to work efficiently without cutting corners on design quality. The result is a process that's faster and more visual than traditional practice, which clients tend to appreciate.

Alongside architecture I'm developing work in wellness and health space design — an area where thoughtful design has real, measurable impact on how people feel. It's the same instinct that drives the residential work: spaces should support the people who use them.
